OVERVIEW

From free SketchUp design to reliable production

ArchiWood keeps SketchUp’s direct, visual modelling approach, so designers can sketch, stretch and talk with the client live. On top of that we add the discipline that factories need: parametric structure, hardware logic, connection rules and CNC output.

For designers ArchiWood provides a free, creative workflow: build any layout with parametric modules, cut around columns or beams, and keep every cabinet editable. Doors and carcasses can be swapped to different materials in one click, so you can explore finish combinations without rebuilding a model.

For manufacturers ArchiWood is a production engine: every panel knows its material, edge banding, drilling and connection type. Once the design is approved you simply run checks, add any remaining hardware and export drawings, reports, nesting and CNC directly for the shop floor.

WORKFLOW

Core workflow & project setup

Define your standards once in the backend, then every new SketchUp project follows the same production logic. From there, layouts, nesting and CNC become repeatable.

Backend configuration & nesting

Global rules for materials, connections and hardware keep every cabinet manufacturable. Nesting layouts are generated directly from the design and are ready to be sent to the machine.

PARAMETRIC EDITING

Fast parametric tools for everyday cabinet work

In ArchiWood the layout remains free, but every cabinet is parametric. Stretching, splitting and re-configuring always a keep stable, production-ready structure.

Quick stretch & smart modules

The idea is simple: place a cabinet, stretch it to size, then refine details using parameter panels. The structure, drilling and hardware all update automatically.

Detailed carcass & front control

Advanced parameters let you handle real-world situations: different back panel styles, mid-rails, plinths, tall units, mixed hardware and more.

SMART RECOGNITION & QUALITY CONTROL

Let the system find spaces, modules and errors for you

ArchiWood analyses room geometry, cabinet structure and tool settings to highlight problems before they reach the CNC. At the same time, smart recognition helps you fill spaces with suitable modules in one click.

Space recognition & labels

Turn a SketchUp room into a smart container: the system recognises openings and walls and suggests cabinets that fit. Labels and QR codes keep panels tracked through production.

Automatic error detection

Before you send a job to nesting, run automatic checks on every panel. ArchiWood reports missing parts, impossible drillings or wrong edge banding so you can fix them instantly.

LIBRARIES & HARDWARE

Parametric libraries that can really go to the shop floor

ArchiWood ships with ready-to-use libraries for cabinet carcasses, doors, panels and hardware. All of them are parametric and can be stretched, re-configured and saved to match your factory standards.

Cabinet libraries

From base units to tall and specialty cabinets, each template contains correct structure and drilling. You extend the library, we keep it parametric.

Automatic hardware & handle placement

Design however you like, then let ArchiWood generate mortises, slots and handles based on your rules. Update rules once and apply them across a whole project.

NESTING & CNC

From elevation to cabinet to CNC in a few clicks

With ArchiWood, a SketchUp elevation can be converted into parametric cabinets, checked, nested and exported to CNC. The whole pipeline is designed for batch size 1.

2D to 3D in one step. Elevations drawn in SketchUp can be converted into full 3D parametric cabinets using ArchiWood tools. You only worry about front dimensions – the system builds the internal structure automatically.

Drawings and documentation. Once the model is finished you can export assembly views, exploded 3D drawings and send everything to LayOut with one command. This removes the need for manual drafting and keeps drawings aligned with the real production data.
